## Challenges

<div class="sp-grid sp-grid-3">
  <article class="sp-card">

    <p>Finding a balance where parts stay in place but can still be repositioned quickly.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="sp-card">
    <h3>Portability vs Durability</h3>
    <p>Making it easy to carry while keeping the pad reliable over repeated usage.</p>
  </article>
  <article class="sp-card">
    <h3>Maintaining Transparency</h3>
    <p>Keeping the surface visually clear enough for alignment and orientation checks.</p>
  </article>
